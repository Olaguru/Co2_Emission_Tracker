About us

Welcome to CO2 Emission Tracker, your go-to platform for monitoring global atmospheric CO2 levels. Our mission is to provide accessible, real-time data on CO2 concentrations to help raise awareness about climate change and its impact on our planet.

Our story

Founded by Oladapo Odedeyi Olayinka, a passionate microbiologist and environmental enthusiast, CO2 EmissionTracker was born out of the need to make critical environmental data available to everyone. With a background in Science Laboratory Technology and experience in various tech projects, Oladapo combined his expertise to develop a platform that empowers individuals, researchers,and organizations to track CO2 emissions effortlessly.

What we do

At CO2 Emission Tracker, we aggregate and visualize CO2 data from reliable sources to help you understand the trends and cycles of atmospheric CO2 levels. Core values

Transparency: We believe in open access to environmental data. Sustainability: Our work is driven by a commitment to preserving the planet. Innovation: We continuously strive to improve our platform and offer the most accurate and up-to-date data.

Our future goals

As we grow, we aim to expand our data offerings and include more environmental metrics. We also plan to develop new tools and features that will make it easier for users to interact with the data and gain meaningful insights.
